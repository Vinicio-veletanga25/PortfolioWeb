Accounting module for the Sicaf ERP system Frontend & Odoo and Python with Docker

The processing time for employee salary payments was a very long process, and it takes a lot of time, due to the lack of management of the employees, their salaries, and days with attended hours.

Requeriments for module

Download and deployment of Docker images

Development of the accounting module in Odoo

Data upload on employee work attendance

Development of the accounting module of the Sicaf ERP system.

The software developed in Python language and applying the accounting logic of Sicaf, generates policies or rules to generate a payment according to the work attendances that each employee has for attended days and relating it to the monthly salary of their hired position.
